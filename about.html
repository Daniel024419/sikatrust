About SikaTrust

SikaTrust is a revolutionary digital financial platform transforming traditional Susu (rotating savings) and microfinance services in Ghana. Founded in 2023 by D&D NextGen Technology, we combine modern fintech solutions with trusted community banking practices.

Our platform digitizes the entire Susu collection and loan management process, bringing transparency, security, and efficiency to group savings. Whether you're an individual saver, a Susu collector, or a microfinance institution, SikaTrust provides the tools you need to grow financially.

With operations in Cape Coast and Takoradi, we're committed to financial inclusion across Ghana, helping thousands manage over GHS 5 million in collective savings since our launch.

Our Mission

To democratize access to financial services by digitizing traditional savings methods while maintaining the trust and community spirit of Ghana's Susu culture. We bridge the gap between informal savings groups and formal banking services.

  • Make financial services accessible to all Ghanaians
  • Digitize traditional savings without losing human connection
  • Provide transparent, real-time financial tracking
  • Offer fair credit opportunities based on savings history
  • Educate communities on financial management

Our Vision

To become Ghana's most trusted digital platform for community finance, where every individual and group can access secure savings tools, fair credit, and financial education - all through their mobile devices.

  • Serve 1 million Ghanaians by 2027
  • Expand to all 16 regions of Ghana
  • Integrate with national digital payment systems
  • Develop AI-powered financial advisory tools
  • Partner with traditional banks to bridge formal/informal sectors
