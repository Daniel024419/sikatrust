Your Trusted Digital Susu Platform

SikaTrust revolutionizes traditional group savings (Susu) by providing a secure digital platform for managing contributions, payouts, and loans. Founded in Ghana, we combine local financial practices with modern technology.

Our platform helps individuals, market women, and small business owners maintain disciplined savings habits while providing access to fair credit based on their savings history. With real-time tracking and mobile money integration, we're bringing financial transparency to community banking.

Why Choose SikaTrust?
  • Secure digital tracking of all contributions and payouts
  • Automated loan management with fair interest rates
  • 24/7 access via mobile app and web portal
  • Integration with Ghana's mobile money services
  • Financial education and growth tools

Our Core Services

Susu Management

Automated rotating savings management with flexible contribution cycles (daily, weekly, monthly) and real-time tracking for individuals and groups.

Loan Services

End-to-end digital loan processing with automated approvals, repayment scheduling, and collection tracking based on savings history.

Financial Analytics

Comprehensive dashboards and reports showing savings trends, loan performance, and group financial health indicators.

Mobile Payments

Seamless integration with mobile money platforms (MTN, Vodafone Cash, AirtelTigo) for easy deposits and withdrawals.

Member Portal

24/7 self-service access for members to check balances, make contributions, request loans, and view transaction history.

Compliance Tools

Automated record-keeping and reporting tools to help groups maintain regulatory compliance and financial transparency.
