Frequently Asked Questions

SikaTrust is a digital Susu (rotating savings) and loan management platform designed to help individuals and groups manage collective savings, loans, and financial analytics securely. It automates contributions, payouts, and reporting.
Sign up on SikaTrust, browse available Susu groups, and request to join. The group administrator will approve your membership. You can also create your own group and invite members.
Loans are approved based on your savings history, group rules, and credit score (if applicable). Admins or automated systems review requests and disburse funds upon approval.
Late contributions may incur penalties (configurable by the group admin). Repeated defaults could restrict loan eligibility or suspend your membership.
Payouts are automated based on the group’s cycle (daily, weekly, etc.). Funds are disbursed to members in a rotating order or via bidding, depending on the group’s settings.
